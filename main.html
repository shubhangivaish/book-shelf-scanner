Titles, authors, and genre are read straight from the photo using Claude's vision. Publisher, ISBN, page count, edition count, cover art, and summaries come from the free, keyless Open Library catalogue via a title/author lookup — coverage is best for widely-published books and may be thin for self-published or regional titles. No live ratings (Amazon/Goodreads don't offer a free public API).
